Story

*Tokyo Revengers* follows Takemichi Hanagaki, a young man who discovers he can travel back in time to his teenage years. After learning that his ex-girlfriend Hinata and her brother are killed by the Tokyo Manji Gang, Takemichi vows to rewrite the past and save them. Along the way, he’s pulled into the dangerous and emotional world of Tokyo’s delinquents.
